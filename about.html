What makes us different

Here, In Prostagers we believe whatever we think we can create. Our team focuses on the solution based on real life problems. We always come up with the innovative ideas to make people lives more and more easier. Our company's main motto is to make world a better place with some piece of code. We already have created some projects solving drainage cleaning problem, air pollution and working on some more awesome projects. Once people became satisfied they stop innovating things, but here we are struggling every day to find problems so that we can discover a solution then to bring that solution to the people. Not only this but our team is participating in many Hackathons not only to win the rewards but to explore the problems out there and to work on a solution.

"We are unstoppable"



Our Goals!

We are here to develop our projects under the name Prostagers. Our goal is to develop prototypes of the solution that will really help people with the problems they face in their daily life. It may include Climate change, health care or automation. we have been a part of various national Hackathon including Smart India Hackathon, hackoverflow 3.0, Dishathon, NASA space app challenge etc. We want to share the knowledge and experience with you and want to help you in making your projects with much ease.
