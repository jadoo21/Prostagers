Introduction

Traditional air quality monitoring methods, such as building air quality monitoring stations, are typical expensive. Even air purification technique is quite expensive. What we are here is to build a device called Planeteer. It will consist of two parts, one part will contain all the monitoring sensors which will be embedded in a small cube box. Other will be a purifier in which we can attach the sensor box. This combined device is an air purifier and monitoring system with some advanced technology which uses IOT and android. In the sake of IOT we are going to use ESP-32 module, MQ-135, MQ-7 sensors to detect the components present in air. Now if we talk about the sensors in detail then MQ-135 sensor is an air quality sensor which will detect the smoke, CO2 and alcohol whereas MQ-7 sensors are going to measure concentration of CO in the air. Then data is sent to your mobile application where the app represents all the data in a graphical and statistical manner with a great UI that is easily understood by the user. This device can be used as two applications. One is we can take the first part of planeteer outside homes so that it can check for air quality outside homes also and suggest the user for a mask if air quality is worst. The other is we can use it in houses with the purifier which will purify the room air when CO concentration or other harmful gases will increase.
